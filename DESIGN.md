# Design System: Roets & Van Rensburg (rvrinc)

## Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Professional, Authoritative, Trustworthy, "Upholding Justice".
- **Visuals**: Premium, Clean, Established but Modern.

## Color Palette
### Primary
- **Deep Navy**: `#0F172A` (Slate-900 equivalent) - *Authority, Stability*
- **Subtle Gold**: `#C5A059` (Metallic/Muted) - *Excellence, Value*
- **Clean White**: `#FFFFFF` - *Clarity*
- **Off-White/Cream**: `#F9FAFB` (Slate-50) - *Backgrounds*

### Secondary
- **Success Green**: `#10B981` (Emerald-500) - *Case Activity*
- **Error Red**: `#EF4444` (Red-500) - *Alerts*
- **Text Dark**: `#1E293B` (Slate-800) - *Readability*
- **Text Muted**: `#64748B` (Slate-500)

## Typography
- **Headings**: **Playfair Display** (Serif) - *Traditional, Elegant*
- **Body/UI**: **Inter** or **Lato** (Sans-serif) - *Modern, Legible*

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary (Action)**: Deep Navy background, White text.
- **Secondary (Info)**: White background, Deep Navy border/text.
- **Accent (Highlight)**: Gold background, White/Navy text.

### Cards
- **Style**: "Modern Professional".
- **Behavior**: Subtle hover lift (`-translate-y-1`), soft shadow (`shadow-lg`).
- **Borders**: Minimalist, occasional Gold top-border for emphasis.

### Navigation
- **Header**: Sticky, White background with shadow.
- **Links**: Deep Navy, bold weight. Gold underline on hover.
- **Mega Menu**: Full-width dropdown for Practice Areas breakdown.
