## üèóÔ∏è 1. The SaaS Business Model (Agency Ownership)
**"I build it, I own it, you use it."**

- **IP Ownership:** All systems, custom code, and AI pipelines designed by Antigravity remain the property of the Developer.
- **Service-Based Access:** Clients do not "buy" the website; they subscribe to the **Antigravity Business Platform**.
- **Termination Clause:** If the monthly service fee is discontinued, access to the platform, admin panels, and custom automations is revoked.
- **Data Portability:** Clients own their *data* (leads, customer info, sales logs), but the *engine* that processes that data belongs to the agency.

## üõ°Ô∏è 2. Core Operational Standards
These are non-negotiable standards applied to every project.

### Data Security & Privacy (POPIA / GDPR)
- **Top of Mind:** Encryption at rest and in transit for all sensitive data.
- **Role-Based Access (RBAC):** Strict separation of Admin, Affiliate, and Client data.
- **Zero-Password Policy:** Prioritize Magic Links or SSO to reduce credential theft risk.

### Backups & Resilience
- **Automated Backups:** Daily database and storage backups via Supabase/Vercel.
- **System Monitoring:** Built-in health checks for AI pipelines (detecting failures in Gemini/Fal.ai calls).
- **Incident Response:** Automated alerts if the site or a critical API route goes down.

## üîÑ 4. The Monthly Innovation Cycle
On the **1st of every month**, a "Project Intelligence Report" is generated for the Designer (You) containing:

1. **New Ideas:** 3 fresh automation or feature ideas based on current market trends.
2. **Implementation Time:** Realistic sprint estimates.
3. **Cost Estimate:** Breakdown of API/Infrastructure increase vs. potential Revenue for the client.
4. **Performance Check:** Summary of "how things are running" (uptime, lead volume, AI success rates).
